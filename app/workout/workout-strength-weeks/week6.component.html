Week 6


Option 1: Skip Week 6. Just use projected one rep max from last week's 1-4 rep set. Start next cycle.
Option 2. Use projected max for next cycle, but take a deload week (repeat week 1 but skip last upper workout).
Option 3. Take this 6th week to actually find your 1 rep max. Then either deload or start new cycle.

Determining Projected Max: Take what you lifted in week 5, and multiply by 1.03 if you completed 2 reps, 1.06 if 3 reps, and 1.09 if 4 reps.
